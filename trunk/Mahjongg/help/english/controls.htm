Controls

Click with left mouse button on tile to select it. Click on mathing tile to remove both.

Click and hold with right mouse button on tile to show matching tiles

Click and hold with right mouse button and move mouse around to look at board from different points. You can also change point of view with cursor keys.

Keys "+" and "-" on the keypad zoom in and out respectively

Press "C" key to bring default point of view
